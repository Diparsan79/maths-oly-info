MATHS OLYMPIAD IN NEPAL

this website is about maths olympiad in nepal!!

here you can learn how to qualify for IMO (international maths olympiad) from nepal :)

THE STAGES TO QUALIFY FOR IMO

there are 4 stages. you have to pass all of them!!


STAGE 1 - DMO (District Maths Olympiad)

this is the first stage. it is held in your district. Its fully MCQ based and its qualifying marks is 20 out of 50 questions


STAGE 2 - PMO (Provincial Maths Olympiad)

this is the second stage. if you pass DMO you come here. it is harder than DMO!! like A LOTTTT HARDERR


STAGE 3 - P-TST (Pre Team Selection Test)

this is the third stage. only good students get till here but this stage is where the real fear starts. Its FULLY PROOF BASED SOLVING.!!


STAGE 4 - TST (Team Selection Test)

this is the FINAL stage!! if you be among the top 6 then you go to IMO!!! CONGRATULATIONS!!!
